About

Serving the people of East Cork for over 35 years An Rothár is East Corks premier cycling centre.
Welcome to An Rothar

An Rothar opened its doors to the public in 1988. Since then we have developed into one of Munster's premier cycle stores specializing in quality bicycles and accessories.

We cater for all aspects of cyclist whether it be a beginner leisure cyclist, or a pure thoroughbred racing cyclist we are your one stop cycle shop. Our trained staff are on hand from 9.15am to 6pm Monday to Saturday to serve you and offer you impartial advice whatever your query may be, to the best of our knowledge and ability.

We pride ourselves on having the ability to pair each customer with the bicycle most suited to their everyday needs. We run a repair service which means you and your bicycle are off the road for minimal time. Drop by and see us for anything you need on two wheels. Whether it’s our very competitive pricing, outstanding service or huge selection, we can guarantee you won't be disappointed.
